Understanding Sleep Efficiency

Sleep Efficiency is calculated as: (Actual Sleep Time ÷ Time Spent in Bed) × 100%

Example:
(7.5h ÷ 8.5h) × 100% = 88.2%

Excellent: 85-100%
Good: 75-84%
Fair: 65-74%
Poor: Below 65%

To improve sleep efficiency:

  • Go to bed only when sleepy
  • Get out of bed if you can't sleep after 20 minutes
  • Use your bed only for sleep (no TV, phone, etc.)
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and large meals before bed
